About The Kalakaaar

Standup comedian. Entertainer. Making the world a happier place one joke at a time, with content that resonates with audiences of all ages, found her passion and love for the stage one day and never went back to being mediocre. Karen started her career as a stand-up comedian, performing at local comedy clubs and open mic events in Bangalore, sharing humorous takes on everyday life, relationships, and cultural observations. Recognizing the potential of digital platforms, Karen expanded her reach by sharing snippets of her stand-up performances on Instagram. Her comedic style blends wit, sarcasm, and relatability, making her stand out in the Indian comedy scene. She often partners with fellow comedians and influencers to create engaging and funny content and has diversified her content by incorporating skits, collaborations, and humorous commentary on societal norms. Her audience appreciates modern, relatable content as every day struggles of a Indian Adult, dealing with your desi parents etc

Our Kalakaaar’s Key Milestones 

She received the Outstanding Influencer Award from SIWAA, acknowledging her impact on social media. She produced 100 videos in a single year, demonstrating her creativity and dedication. Featured on platforms like the “UNDERCITY” podcast, she shares insights into her journey and experiences
